CHAPTER: MOLE CONCEPT
- Always remember: 1 mole = 6.022 x 10^23 particles
- Molar mass of water = 18 g/mol
- Most common mistake students make: forgetting to 
  convert ml to litres in molarity questions

IMPORTANT TIPS FROM SUKESH SIR:
- Always write units in every step of numericals
- NCERT examples are the most important for boards
- Practice PYQs from last 5 years minimum
